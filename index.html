Workshop Learning Outcomes

By completing these workshops, you will be able to:

  • Apply University of Glasgow GenAI policies in your teaching practice
  • Create comprehensive, scientifically accurate biology content using AI tools
  • Design accessible learning materials that meet WCAG 2.1 AA standards
  • Transform traditional lectures into interactive, engaging experiences
  • Develop quality control processes for AI-generated educational content
  • Integrate advanced prompting techniques for laboratory preparation and safety protocols
